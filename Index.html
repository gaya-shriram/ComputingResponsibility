Quick Overview


The right to be forgotten is a concept discussed and put into practice in the European Union (EU) and Argentina since 2006.The issue has arisen from desires of individuals to "determine the development of their life in an autonomous way, without being perpetually or periodically stigmatized as a consequence of a specific action performed in the past. This is an interesting topic and an eye opener on our rights and how to be safe.
